About Aaeronix Engineering

Pioneering Efficiency and Reliability in Air Movement Technology.

Our Commitment to Excellence

Aaeronix Engineering, established in 2024 in Palghar, Maharashtra, is a leading manufacturer of industrial air handling and ventilation solutions. We specialize in designing and delivering high-performance products such as Cyclone Dust Collectors, Axial Flow Fans, Industrial Blowers, Exhaust Blowers, Extraction Units, and Ducting Services.

Our process emphasizes using premium materials from trusted vendors, skilled craftsmanship adhering to quality standards, and rigorous testing to ensure defect-free products for every client.
