Our Story

Pioneering Efficiency in Air Movement Technology

Aaeronix Engineering, established in 2024 in Palghar, Maharashtra, is a leading manufacturer of industrial air handling solutions. We specialize in high-performance Cyclone Dust Collectors, Axial Flow Fans, and Industrial Blowers.
